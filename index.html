About

Wilson Creek Primitive Baptist Church is located in Arrington, TN. We were established in 1971. We enjoy worshiping God according to Matthew 10:14.

Live Streaming

Join us each Sunday at 10am (central) as we live stream our service. You will hear a timely Word delivered by Pastor Grahm Sims. Video is streamed from our Facebook feed.

What to expect

When a person first visits a Primitive Baptist Church, they will notice a simple worship service which consists of congregational singing, prayer and preaching. There will also be an absence of musical instruments, choirs, Sunday Schools and other auxiliary functions, which is in keeping with the pattern of New Testament worship.
